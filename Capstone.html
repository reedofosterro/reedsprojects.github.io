Yogi Tea Predictive Sales Model and Analysis

Project Details

Worked in groups of 5.

Yogi Tea is one of America's largest tea companies. They price their tea at a premium and are sold nationwide. We were asked to help find the following information for Yogi Tea:

  • What is their Price Elasticity Coefficient
  • What is the lift % in sales by running a Temporary Price Reduction (TPR)
  • What is the lift % in sales by running a Feature Promotion
  • What is the lift % in sales by running a Display Promotion

Additionally, Yogi Tea wanted a model that could predict their unit sales for a specific geography. Finding these datapoints will help Yogi Tea forecast the financial impacts of different price points and aid in decision making for promotion usage. This notebook shares our report on finding this information, building a model, and additional recommendations for Yogi Tea.

Due to an NDA agreement, we are not permitted to disclose any specific findings or data we worked with. The below links are empty. Limited details can be shared per request.

Ultimately, we constructed a linear regression model (that accounted for seasonality, geography, and more), and 4 different sets of code to compute elasticity coefficients for general price, TPR (total price reduction), Feature promotions, and Display promotions. These things helped us provide valuable insights into the companies pricing and promotional tactis in different locations throughout the US.
