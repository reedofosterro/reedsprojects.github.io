Life expectancy regression analysis

Project Details

Worked in groups of four

Goal of project: Perform a regression analysis on a topic of our choosing, remove collinearity and come up with a final model.

Conducted a comprehensive regression analysis that correlates human life expectancy with critical variables. The conclusions offer valuable insights into a country's developmental status. I personally helped with cleaning the data set and every aspect of the analysis in our movement towards a final model. I also covered slides 3, 7, and 13 in the presentation of the findings from our analysis.
