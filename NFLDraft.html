NFL Draft analysis

Project Details

Worked in groups of five.

Led a team in conducting a comprehensive analysis of NFL Draft data from 2000 to 2015 using SQL and database management techniques. Designed and implemented a relational database schema, normalized data, and executed complex SQL queries to answer key research questions related to draft picks, player performance, and positional trends. Developed a web application in Oracle APEX to showcase analysis results and provide user-friendly access to the dataset.

In terms of my personal involement, I was involved in all aspects of coming up with our initial topic and the associated introduction, data details, and research questions outlined in the project proposal and eventual report. Each of us were responsible for answering one question, which involved the associated code, web page visualization, and final report workings. I completed research question 4 by myself and helped out with question 1 largely as well.

# QUESTION 4
SELECT FirstName, LastName, rush_yds as "Rushing Yards"
FROM PLAYER
JOIN DRAFT ON PLAYER.PlayerID = DRAFT.PlayerID 
WHERE DRAFT.Draft_Year BETWEEN 2000 AND 2015 AND rush_yds = (SELECT MAX(rush_yds) FROM PLAYER)
FETCH FIRST 1 ROWS ONLY
;
